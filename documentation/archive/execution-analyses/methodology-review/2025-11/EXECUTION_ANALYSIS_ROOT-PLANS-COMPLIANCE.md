## üîç Key Findings

### Finding 1: Most PLANs Missing Project Context Reference

**Observation**: 9 of 13 PLANs (69.2%) are missing the Project Context reference in "Context for LLM Execution" section.

**Implication**: LLMs executing these PLANs may not have access to essential project knowledge, leading to context gaps.

**Action Required**: Add Project Context reference to all 9 PLANs in Achievement 1.1.

---

### Finding 2: Focus Rules Missing in Most PLANs

**Observation**: 8 of 13 PLANs (61.5%) are missing "What to Read (Focus Rules)" section.

**Implication**: LLMs may read too much context, causing context overload and slower execution.

**Action Required**: Add Focus Rules section to all 8 PLANs in Achievement 1.1.

---

### Finding 3: Size Limits Not Documented

**Observation**: 8 of 13 PLANs (61.5%) are missing "Size Limits" section.

**Implication**: No clear guidance on PLAN size constraints, risking PLANs that exceed methodology limits.

**Action Required**: Add Size Limits section to all 8 PLANs in Achievement 1.1.

---

### Finding 4: GrammaPlan Consideration Missing

**Observation**: 8 of 13 PLANs (61.5%) are missing "GrammaPlan Consideration" section.

**Implication**: No documentation of whether GrammaPlan was considered, making it unclear if work should have been split.

**Action Required**: Add GrammaPlan Consideration section to all 8 PLANs in Achievement 1.1.

---

### Finding 5: Two PLANs Fully Compliant

**Observation**: 2 PLANs are 100% compliant (PLAN_FILE-MOVING-ADVANCED-OPTIMIZATION.md, PLAN_TESTING-REQUIREMENTS-ENFORCEMENT.md).

**Implication**: These PLANs serve as good examples for template compliance.

**Action Required**: Use these as reference when updating other PLANs.

---

### Finding 6: Meta-PLANs Have Different Structure

**Observation**: PLAN_METHODOLOGY-V2-ENHANCEMENTS.md and PLAN_STRUCTURED-LLM-DEVELOPMENT.md have different missing sections (Goal, Problem Statement vs. Focus Rules, Size Limits).

**Implication**: Meta-PLANs may have intentional structure differences. Need to verify if missing sections are intentional.

**Action Required**: Review meta-PLANs separately - may need different compliance criteria.
